This score contains an audio DSP.

It will interact with the microphone and audio of the connected clients. Some parameters of this Audio DSP will change automatically when the cursor enters SECTIONS represented by numbered markers. (1, 2, 3, 4) To trigger theses events manually, you can press theses keyboard shortcuts:

Activate section 2 2

Activate section 4 4
